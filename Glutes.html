GLUTE BRIDGE Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat on the floor, lift your hips to form a straight line from shoulders to knees, then lower. DONKEY KICKS On all fours, kick one leg back and upward, keeping the knee bent at 90 degrees, then lower and repeat on the other side. FIRE HYDRANTS On all fours, lift one leg out to the side while keeping the knee bent, then lower and repeat on the other side. SQUATS Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body as if sitting back in a chair, then stand back up. LUNGES Step forward with one leg, lower your body into a 90-degree angle at both knees, then push back to the starting position and alternate legs. WALL SIT Sit against a wall with your knees at a 90-degree angle, hold the position for as long as you can to engage the glutes. CLAMSHELL Lie on your side with knees bent, open and close your knees while keeping your feet together to activate the glutes and hip abductors.
