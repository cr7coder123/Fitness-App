PUSH UPS Keep your body in a straight line, lower yourself until your chest almost touches the floor, then push back up. INCLINE PUSH UPS Place your hands on an elevated surface (like a table) and perform push-ups to target the lower chest. DECLINE PUSH UPS Elevate your feet on a surface and do push-ups to emphasize the upper chest. WIDE PUSH UPS Place your hands wider than shoulder-width to engage the outer chest more. DIAMOND PUSH UPS Place your hands close together forming a diamond shape to target the inner chest and triceps. ARCHER PUSH UPS Extend one arm out while lowering your chest toward the other arm to increase chest activation. CLAPPING PUSH UPS Start in a standard push-up position, lower your chest to the ground, then explosively push up so your hands leave the floor and clap before landing back in the starting position.
