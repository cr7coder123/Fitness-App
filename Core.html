CRUNCHES Lie on your back, knees bent, curl your upper body toward your knees, then lower slowly. LEG RAISES Lie on your back, legs straight, lift them to a 90-degree angle, then slowly lower without touching the floor. RUSSIAN TWISTS Sit on the floor, lean back slightly, rotate your torso side to side, tapping the floor each time. PLANK Hold a forearm or full plank position, keeping your body straight from head to heels. MOUNTAIN CLIMBERS In a plank position, drive one knee toward your chest, alternating quickly.
