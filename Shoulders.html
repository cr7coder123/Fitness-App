PIKE PUSH UPS Start in a downward dog position, lower your head toward the floor, then push back up to target the shoulders. DECLINE PIKE PUSH UPS Elevate your feet on a surface to increase difficulty and focus more on the shoulders. SHOULDER TAPS In a push-up position, lift one hand to tap the opposite shoulder, alternating sides to build stability and strength. HANDSTAND HOLD Kick up against a wall and hold for as long as possible to build shoulder endurance and strength. ARM CIRCLES Extend your arms and make small and large circles to warm up and strengthen the shoulders. PULL UPS Grip a pull-up bar slightly wider than shoulder-width, pull yourself up until your chin clears the bar, then lower slowly.
