GLUTE BRIDGE Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat on the floor, lift your hips to form a straight line from shoulders to knees, then lower. MOUNTAIN CLIMBERS In a plank position, drive one knee toward your chest, alternating legs quickly to engage the hamstrings. LATERAL LEG SWINGS Stand on one leg, swing the other leg back and forth to engage the hamstrings and hip flexors. STANDING LEG CURLS Stand and bend one knee to bring your heel toward your glutes, then lower and repeat with the other leg.
